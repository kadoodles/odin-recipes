S'mores Recipe

Description o' S'mores

In my kitchen, you won't see a single s'more, ever. Why? I believe in the authentic s'more: one crisped and blackened to gooey perfection in the heat of a handmade campfire. In my kitchen, there are no fires, therefore no s'mores. That's just me. You do you. Perhaps you enjoy a microwave-melted, crispless marshmallow.. a gooey blob upon your cracker of graham, consumed under your dimmable LED kitchen lights. Not I! I believe in the heart of the s'more, a flavor which is only tasted in the open air of night. Come with me! Follow my recipe, and I will deliver you not only choclate, graham, and marshmallow.. I will deliver you a taste of joy.

Ingredients

Servings: 1 Prep Time: 2 mins Cook Time: 2 mins
  • Graham Crackers (2)
  • Milk Chocolate Bar (1)
  • Marshmallow (1)
  • Campfire
  • Long stick
  • Night Sky (with or without stars)
  • Friends and/or Family (optional but suggested)

Steps

  1. Take a moment to enjoy the fresh air and company
  2. Stab marshmallow with stick
  3. Roast marshmallow over campfire until desired level of crisp is achieved
  4. Use graham cracker to pull hot marshmallow from stick
  5. Add chocolate to remaining graham cracker
  6. Assemble as pictured and eat warm
