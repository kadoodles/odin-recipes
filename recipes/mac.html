Kraft Mac n Cheese Recipe

The One, the Only, Mac

Try asking my son Mi what he wants to eat, any day/any time I can guarantee he will say "Mac!" It was one of his first words. He's totally obsessed. He's only 18 months old, but I think he'll be craving mac for at least the next 13 years. I should clarify: by "mac" I mean, more specifically, Kraft Mac 'n Cheese original. Sure, homemade mac is great, probably better for your arteries.. but Kraft is the OG. Now, making Kraft Mac is a pretty complicated artform, usually only attempted by the most talented chefs. I believe my recipe has sufficently dumbed down the process, so follow along closely and you should be fine.

Ingredients

Servings: See package Prep Time: See package Cook Time: See package
  • Box of Kraft Mac n Cheese Original
  • Milk (the box says milk is optional, but it is in fact not optional)
  • Butter (very optional)
  • Siracha (for the spicy girlies)

Steps

  1. Shake mac n cheese box and have a mac dance with your toddler
  2. Follow instructions on box
  3. If spicy girlie, add siracha
  4. Eat up buttercup
